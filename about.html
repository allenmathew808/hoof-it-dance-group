Our Story

Founded in 2017, Hoof-it DC started as a group of passionate street performers brought together by their love for movement and rhythm. What began as sidewalk freestyles and underground battles has evolved into a high-energy, professional dance crew known for electrifying live shows and viral social media content.

The group quickly gained attention for their fusion of hip-hop, contemporary, and Afrobeat choreography. Over the years, Hoof-it DC has toured nationally, collaborated with music artists, and been featured in dance festivals across North America.

Today, Hoof-it DC remains committed to pushing boundaries, mentoring new dancers, and celebrating diversity through dance.
